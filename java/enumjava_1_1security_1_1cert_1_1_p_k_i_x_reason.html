Detailed Description

The

PKIXReason

enumerates the potential PKIX-specific reasons that an X.509 certification path may be invalid according to the PKIX (RFC 3280) standard. These reasons are in addition to those of the

CertPathValidatorException.BasicReason

enumeration.
